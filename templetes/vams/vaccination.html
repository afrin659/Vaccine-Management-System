Vaccination Program of VaxCare

Vaccines are very important for your child; it protects your child against various dreaded diseases like tetanus, diphtheria, pertussis (whooping cough), polio, measles, hepatitis B, haemophilus influenza type b (which causes some meningitis and pneumonia), yellow fever, tuberculosis, etc. Apollo Vaccination Centre offers a range of vaccinations not only for your child, but also for adults and travellers. Patients are immunized at our Centre as per the Extended Program of Immunization (EPI) of World Health Organization (WHO) protocols.
